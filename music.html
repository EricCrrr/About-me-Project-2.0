🎵 My Favorite Music

Listening to music and going to concerts are one of my favortie things to do. Even though concerts are expensive it is worth it in my opinion since live music sounds so much better. Music has the power to bring people together as many people can relate to either a song or to an entire album.

Don Toliver

Don Toliver has evolved so much since I first started listening to him. His newest album Octane has been in rotation for the last couple months and is contender for album of the year. Apart from Octane his last album Hardstone Psycho was also really good and had amazing prodcution as well.

Listen on Spotify

Lauryn Hill

Lauren Hill has also been in rotation this year. Her production is top tier along with being really catchy. The song that has been in my rotaion has been Killing me softly with his songs. It's dissapointing to know that she probably will never release another album.

Kali Uchis

Kali Uchis was an artist that I got into during the pandemic. Her voice along with her production are always really and she's been really consistent in terms of dropping music. I have also seen her twice live and she did an amazing job coming up with set designs and set list.
